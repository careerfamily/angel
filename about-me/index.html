Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit more about me

My name is Angel, I am a mother of two from Fishers, Indiana.

Being a stay-at-home parent is rewarding and the hardest job I have experienced, yet. The skills I gained from the experience are easily transferable.

Mastermind of turning chaos into structure!

My love of structuring daily routines stems from the experience of being a stay-at-home parent.

I am the person who thrives on organizing special events. I love making people’s lives easier by unburdening them.

The talent of multi-tasking and organization led to many delightful projects and hobbies.

  • Directing a Summer Camp
  • Blogging
  • Food Photography
  • Cake decorating
